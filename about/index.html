About Us

Christ Redeemer's International Secondary School is a Government Approved Christian Secondary School owned by The Redeemed Christian Church of God, Rivers Province 6.

Our school is concerned with raising godly children through a holistic approach and transformation of our children into academically and spiritually balanced individuals. We are equipped with modern teaching and educational tools that make learning easy.

Notably, we have diligent and passionate academic and non-academic staff as well as a stimulating learning environment. Undoubtedly, the school provides enriched and broad learning opportunities for all students. We offer an intensive and internationally recognised curriculum in addition to other extracurricular activities which produce outstanding results in our students.

Above all, your interest in the school is quite appreciated as we believe that our prospectus will guide you in making an informed decision regarding your child. We are hopeful that we would see your child at Christ Redeemer's International Secondary School. Register with us today!
